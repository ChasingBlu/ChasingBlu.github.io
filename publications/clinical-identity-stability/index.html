Abstract

This clinical whitepaper extends the Recursive Emotional Contextual Patterning framework toward clinical interpretation. It proposes identity stability as a measurable dynamical field and introduces a clinical bridge between computational identity-field measurements and future interdisciplinary work in neuroscience, psychiatry, addiction research, dissociative conditions, and neurodegenerative disease.
